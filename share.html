Share Your Voice

Art and poetry can illuminate truths that facts and numbers alone cannot

History lives not just in textbooks, but in the stories we tell, the art we create, and the truths we refuse to let fade. Your voice matters in this ongoing conversation.

We invite you to share your own art, poetry, music, or reflections on the history of racism in America. Whether it's a family story passed down through generations, a piece inspired by what you've learned, or a vision for the future you want to build. Your perspective adds to the mosaic.

Post on your social media with the hashtag:

#WhereWallsFall

Together, we create a chorus of voices that cannot be silenced. Every share, every story, every piece of art chips away at the walls that divide us.

Walls are built by fear. They fall when we choose to see each other.
